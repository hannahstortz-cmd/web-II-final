About Kihm's Bakery

We are four homeschooled sisters who love baking. We wanted to open a bakery with a name that represents all of us, that's why we chose the name Kihm's. Each letter represents one of our names (Kayla, Isabella, Hannah, and Maya).

We bake and attend small farmer's markets but we love to bake our items fresh for you when you order them through our website.
